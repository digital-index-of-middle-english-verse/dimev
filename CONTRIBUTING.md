We welcome corrections and additions to DIMEV content.
The best way to contribute is to [create an "issue"](https://docs.github.com/en/issues/tracking-your-work-with-issues/using-issues/creating-an-issue).
The next-best way is to email one of the current custodians of DIMEV, Ian Cornelius or Mike Johnston.

Contributors should be aware that content corrections will not be implemented soon: these are on hold while we review and modernize DIMEV's data structures and management practices.
See the [documentation](https://github.com/digital-index-of-middle-english-verse/dimev/releases/latest/download/documentation.pdf).
